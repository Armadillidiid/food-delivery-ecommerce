Depending on how many locations you have, it’s possible to become an Rumble restaurant partner and start accepting orders in just a few days! You can begin the process by signing up here. We’re excited to hear from you!

Rumble pricing has two parts. A one-time activation fee sets restaurants up with a welcome kit, tablet, restaurant software, and professional photo shoot. A service fee is calculated as a percentage of each restaurant order made through Rumble. Want more details? Email restaurants@uber.com and we’ll get back to you.

The Rumble platform can connect you with independent drivers, bike and scooter riders, and walkers who deliver to your customers. Because of the network of delivery people using the Rumble platform, restaurants don’t have to keep their own delivery staff. But if you do have your own staff, we’re flexible—you can use them too. Reach out to restaurants@Rumble.com or directly to your Rumble Eats contact to see if this option is now available in your city.
